Account Deletion

Delete your PractEar account and data

This page explains how PractEar users can request deletion of their account and associated app data.

How to request deletion

  1. Open the PractEar app.
  2. Go to Profile, then Account Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.

If you cannot access the app, you can request deletion by emailing us from the email address connected to your PractEar account.

Email account deletion request

Data deleted after verification

  • Your PractEar account profile and account identifiers.
  • Practice progress, exercise history, stats, and leaderboard data associated with your account.
  • User-provided feedback or support data that can reasonably be linked to your account, unless retention is required for legal, security, or support reasons.

Data that may be retained

Some limited records may be retained when required for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, or payment compliance purposes. App Store, Google Play, and payment provider purchase records may be retained by those providers according to their own policies.

Where retention is required, PractEar will keep the data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the relevant purpose.

Processing timeline

We aim to respond to account deletion requests within a reasonable time. We may ask you to verify account ownership before deletion is completed.
